About me

My name is Ninh (Vincent) DO. I am a new doctoral graduate in Engineering with Emphasis on Computational and Data Science.

I am currently working as a Machine Learning engineer for a start-up company in public health. The work is challenging but also rewarding with the meaningful moments of helping people identify potential medical conditions.

I am particularly interested in applying technologies to solving real-world problems. I continuously make every effort to diversify my skills for the future career. Of my current interests are back-end and full-stack developments, or how to build machine learning apps from end to end. This playground is to serve a part of my learning course.

I enjoy exploring new things in my free time. Those include a well-reviewed book, an unfamiliar place, a new movie or a newly open restaurant. I love math, gym, swimming, investment, plants, and dogs. I like Japanese food and culture. I value compassion, transparency, freedom and diversity. I am a direct and open-minded guy who is open to different lifestyles without judgement.
